Abdullah Talayhan - Personal Webpage

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Bilkent University Computer Engineering Department. Previously, I got my PhD at EPFL LASEC, Switzerland. I have graduated from Bilkent University, Turkey with a degree in Computer Engineering. Currently working on post-quantum cryptography and zero-knowledge proofs.
